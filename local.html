Sammamish Commons Park

Sammamish Commons Park is a popular community park in Sammamish, Washington. It features a blend of open green spaces, wooded areas, and well-maintained trails. The park offers a variety of recreational amenities, including sports fields, playgrounds, picnic areas, and walking paths that provide scenic views of the surrounding natural beauty. It also houses a community garden and a skate park, making it a favorite destination for families and outdoor enthusiasts. Sammamish Commons is often used for local events and gatherings, offering a peaceful setting with a connection to the area's natural environment.

Yellow Lake Park

The Yellow Lake Loop Trail, located in the Klahanie neighborhood of Sammamish, Washington, is a serene and accessible nature trail that circles the scenic Yellow Lake. This loop trail offers an easy walk through a lush, forested environment, with views of the calm lake at its center. The path is well-maintained, making it suitable for walkers of all ages and skill levels. Along the trail, visitors can enjoy the natural beauty of the Pacific Northwest, including native plants, birds, and other wildlife. It's a peaceful spot for locals to relax, take a leisurely stroll, or walk their dogs. The trail provides a refreshing break from the suburban surroundings and is especially beautiful during the fall when the foliage changes color.

Big Rock Park

Big Rock Park is a scenic and peaceful park located in Sammamish, Washington. The park is known for its natural beauty, featuring towering trees, open meadows, and the park's namesake—an enormous glacial erratic, or "big rock," that serves as a geological point of interest. Visitors to Big Rock Park can enjoy hiking along well-maintained trails that wind through forests and wetlands, offering a quiet retreat in nature. The park also has picnic areas, interpretive signs, and educational opportunities, making it a family-friendly destination. It's a favorite for those looking to explore the outdoors, go birdwatching, or simply enjoy a tranquil setting.
